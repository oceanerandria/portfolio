For our Design 1 class, we were given an assignment to design an album cover for a fictional music band The design cover here represents the Kenyan flag, which hints the music genre, African Pop. The album name 'a few people can handle it' is a tribute to woe=men, especially mothers. This is why the centerpiece of the flag is replaced with the silhouette of a woman.
